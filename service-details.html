CONSERVATION PHILOSOPHY

Stabilizing the Past, Protecting the Future

Our Master Restoration service is designed for the most significant historical artifacts. We don't just "fix" firearms; we perform a forensic restoration that halts environmental decay while preserving every original maker's mark and tool signature.

Whether it's a 17th-century matchlock or a rare Civil War revolver, our process ensures that the mechanical integrity is restored using period-correct materials and chemical browning techniques that match the original factory specifications.

Non-invasive chemical stabilization
Period-correct metal blueing & browning
Master-level cabinetry & stock repair
TECHNICAL WORKFLOW

The Restoration Lifecycle

01. Stabilization

Identifying and arresting active oxidation and fungal decay in wood.

02. Metallurgy

Cold-rust browning and charcoal blueing to match original finishes.

03. Cabinetry

Hand-rubbed oil finishes and precision wood-to-metal fitting.

04. Dossier

Complete digital documentation and historical verification report.
