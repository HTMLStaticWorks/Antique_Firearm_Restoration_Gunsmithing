METALLURGY & FINISHING

The Art of the Deep Blue

Historical blueing is not just a coating; it's a chemical transformation. Our master smiths use period-correct slow-rust blueing techniques that can take over 100 hours of labor to achieve the correct museum-grade depth and luster.

  • Slow-rust and Nitre blueing processes
  • Precision micro-welding for pitting repair
  • Historical chemical analysis of original finishes
CABINETRY & CHECKERING
